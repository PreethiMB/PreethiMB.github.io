About me

Spatial thinker,
data storyteller.

I'm Preethi, a geospatial professional with a passion for understanding how place shapes everything around us. I use tools like GIS, remote sensing, and spatial analysis to solve real-world problems.

From urban planning to environmental monitoring, I love turning raw geographic data into actionable insights — and capturing the beauty of the natural world through photography.
